Our Programs

Choose the path that suits your goals. All levels welcome!

👶 Little Dragons (Ages 4–7)

Introduce your child to the basics of martial arts in a fun and playful environment. Focus on discipline, coordination, and self-control.

🧒 Junior Warriors (Ages 8–13)

Structured training for kids with a balance of fitness, self-defense, confidence building, and respect for others.

🧑‍🦱 Teen & Adult Program

Perfect for beginners and experienced martial artists. Improve your fitness, technique, and mental strength.

🛡️ Self-Defense Classes

Learn effective and practical techniques to defend yourself. Real-world scenarios, taught with a safety-first mindset.

🏅 Tournament Team

Train and compete at local, national, and international martial arts tournaments. For students who want to take it to the next level!
